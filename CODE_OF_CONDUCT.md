# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give and receive feedback with care.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about problems.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Public or private abuse.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants whose behavior is harmful to the project community.
